    You Ask - We Answer

    -

    Can the Mac & PC Doctors upgrade my computer?

    Apple Inc. does not manufacture any parts. They are only a design and engineering company. The parts Apple uses in the iPhone are bought from Chinese vendors and assembled in China. Many of the parts we use come from similar vendors in China. So though we cannot claim that our parts are "from Apple".

    +

    Does my Apple warranty cover its problems?

    Our water damage repair service includes a 90-day warranty so if you experience any water-related issues caused by the initial water damaged within 90-days, you are covered. All repairs carry 90-day parts and labor warranty which means if you have any issues related to the repair within 90 days we will take care of it free of charge!

    +

    How long will my repair take?

    Almost all repairs can be completed while you wait in 30 minutes or less by one of our experienced technicians. The only repair that cannot be completed while you wait is the water damaged repair service. Water damaged devices generally need to be dropped off for at least 24-48 hours.

    +

    How does your warranty plan work?

    We offer a 90 day warranty for all Apple products, and a 30 day warranty for Samsung phones. Our warranty covers our parts and workmanship. If the repaired or replaced parts stop working properly, bring it back and we will redo the repair at no cost to you.
